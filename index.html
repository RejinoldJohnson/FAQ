FAQ - Resume Questions

  • Should I include a photograph on my resume?

    It depends on the industry and the job you’re applying for. In most cases, it's best to omit it to avoid potential bias.

  • Is it important to include my home address on my resume?

    It’s no longer necessary to include your full home address. Listing your city and state is often sufficient.

  • Should I add links to my LinkedIn profile or portfolio on my resume?

    Yes! Including links to your LinkedIn profile, portfolio, or relevant online work can enhance your resume.

  • Is it acceptable for my resume to exceed one page?

    It can be, especially for professionals with extensive experience. Aim for clarity and conciseness, though.

  • What should I do if I have gaps in my employment history?

    Address the gaps honestly in your cover letter, and focus on skills and experiences gained during that time.
